About Us

Xplora Club provides students with great opportunities which helps them grow their knowledge and skills in various fields. We do this by conducting workshops and seminars which enable them to contribute to the open source project and communities. They gain relevant experience by solving problems for local organizations with technology based solutions. The advantage we possess is that anyone with good knowledge can demonstrate it, since this club does not have any restrictions on the conductee.
